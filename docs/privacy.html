Veyronix Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-21

Veyronix Studio ("Veyronix") provides tools to generate short-form videos from user prompts and publish them to connected social media accounts after explicit user approval.

Information We Process

  • OAuth connection data required to connect your social media accounts.
  • Video prompts and related metadata.
  • Generated video files and upload status logs.

How We Use Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Authenticate and connect your accounts (e.g., TikTok, YouTube).
  • Generate videos using integrated video providers.
  • Upload videos only after user confirmation.

Third-Party Services

Veyronix integrates with third-party services such as TikTok, YouTube, and video generation providers. Data necessary to perform requested actions may be transmitted to these services.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate the service, maintain logs, and provide requested functionality.

Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect stored access tokens and user data.

Your Rights

You may request account data deletion by contacting us.
